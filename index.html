Daniel D. R. Barros

Computer Scientist

I conduct research at the intersection of Software Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, and Inclusive Education, exploring how intelligent and well-engineered systems can improve accessibility and learning experiences.

I am a Computer Scientist with extensive experience in software engineering, technical leadership, and global technology delivery. After a career in the financial industry building and managing complex systems in São Paulo and London, I transitioned to academia to focus on AI and inclusive learning technologies. I hold an MSc in Computer Science (UFABC) and received the Distinguished Paper Award at CBSoft/SBES 2021. I am currently pursuing a PhD researching how Artificial Intelligence and Computer Science can help create more accessible, adaptive, and equitable educational environments.

🔬 Research

  • Software Engineering for AI Systems: Designing robust and scalable architectures for intelligent platforms.
  • Artificial Intelligence in Education: Applying AI to support adaptive learning and educational tools.
  • Inclusive and Accessible Learning Technologies: Developing AI-driven solutions for diverse and neurodivergent learners.
